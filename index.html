About Me

After spending several years in restaurant management and finding it ultimately unfulfilling, I decided it was time to find a career I could truly be passionate about. To that end, I re-enrolled in college and earned a bachelor's degree in computer science. I am now looking for employment in the tech field so I can start the next chapter of my life.

While I am extremely happy with the education I received at Cal State, there is only so much one can learn in the classroom. I am now working to identify where my education is lacking and finding the resources needed to learn the relevant skills. Currently, I am taking online courses to become more proficient with git and GitHub and learn to use the advanced debugging features of my favorite IDE's as well as continuing to work on two projects from my senior year at CSUN.

While my work experience in tech is on par with other recent graduates, I believe the management experience and communication skills I honed in the service industry, combined with my natural aptitude for logical thinking and love of problem solving, allow me to bring a unique tool set to the table.

Current Projects

Below are two projects which began as group projects during my senior year at CSUN. They have now become personal ventures that I continue to work on as a way to learn new programming techniques. A brief description of each is provided along with a link to a website if you would like a more in-depth look.

VR Drums

Current research overwhelmingly shows the benefits of music education for children. Unfortunately, music and arts are often the first things that get cut from the curriculum when a school district finds itself short on funding. The VR Drums project aims to create a cheap, portable, and quiet means of keeping music education in the classroom.

  • VR Drums Website

Kinetic Radiance

In recent years, the market for products that enourage mindfulness and relaxation has proven to be substantial. Adult coloring books, apps that offer guided meditation, and YouTube channels streaming videos of peaceful locales remain in high demand. Kinetic Radiance combines music and visually stunning effects in a virtual reality environment to offer the user an easily accessible escape from reality that engages the senses.
